Best Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

To make the best chocolate chip cookies we have to prepare to get: Butter, Sugar, Brown sugar, Large eggs, Vanilla, Baking Power, Salt and Chocolate Chip

  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Brown Sugar
  • Large eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Baking Power
  • Salt and Chocolate Chip

Instructions

To actually make the cookies, following the steps:

  • Preheat oven to 375'F
  • Cream 1 cup softened butter with 1 cup white sugar and 1 cup brown sugar
  • Add 2 eggs and 2 teaspoons vanilla extract, and mix well
  • In a separate bowl, combine 3 cups flour, 1 teaspoon baking soda, 1/2 teaspoon baking power, and 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Gradually mix the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients
  • Stir in 2 cups chocolate chips
  • Scoop dough onto a banking sheet
  • Bake for 8-10 mins untol edges are golden
  • Let cool and enjoy!

Ingredients Quantity
Unsalted butter 226g
Sugar 200g
Brown sugar 220g
Large eggs 2
Vanilla 2 teaspoons
Baking power 1/2 teaspoon
Salt 1/2
chocolate chip 2 cup
